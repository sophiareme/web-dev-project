Really Quick...What are "Zodiacs?"

To put it simply, in astrology, zodiacs are directly related to the twelve specific constellations of the zodiac that the sun passes through (listed above). What makes these specific bunches of stars so popular, is the belief that it connects to ones personality and overall influence of someones life, as a zodiac is determined by your birthday. These special twelve were created by the Babylonians in 1894 BC. The belief of atrolgy spread all over, but our modern way of using it comes from the Greeks, who adopted the study along the line and incorperated it to connect with a person.

Here are the twelve zodiacs in order, find yours!

  1. Aries: March 21 - April 19
  2. Taurus: April 20 - May 20
  3. Gemini: May 21 - June 20
  4. Cancer: June 21 - July 22
  5. Leo: July 23 - August 22
  6. Virgo: August 23 - September 22
  7. Libra: September 23 - October 22
  8. Scorpio: October 23 - November 21
  9. Sagittarius: November 22 - December 21
  10. Capricorn: December 22 - January 19
  11. Aquarius: January 20 - February 18
  12. Pisces: February 19 - March 20

Now About Mine

I was born on November 17, making me a Scorpio. Scorpio, (whos symbol is the scorpion) is the eigth sign of the zodiacs. Its origins tracing back to the scorpius constellation. Now really quick, all zodiac are under four different elements -- Earth,Fire,Air, and Water. Scorpios are water signs. Personality wise, this means we are quite imaginative,compassionate, but often very sensitive and moody.





Like all the other zodiacs, scorpios have Alot too them. Their most common traits are:

  • Loyal
  • Smart
  • Determined
  • Emotional/Intense
  • Honest (maybe even...TOO honest
  • Jealous
  • Secretive
  • Stubborn




Its safe to say that scorpios can be PRETTY complex and intense. They are very strict about their goals and will do everything to make them met. While it can be seen as a positive...It can be pretty toxic as we arent very comfy with change. Scorpios also happened to be fixed sign (one of the four along with Taurus,Leo, and Aquarius) When fixed signs find their comfy spot, its almost impossible to get out of it. Fixed signs tend have very independent mindsets
